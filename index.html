About Me

I'm a passionate 3D Texture Artist with expertise in the game and movie industries. I specialize in creating realistic, grungy texture work using Substance Painter, Substance Designer, and Unreal Engine. My focus is on crafting immersive, detailed environments and props that tell compelling stories through meticulous surface detailing.

With a keen eye for material authenticity and weathering effects, I bring assets to life with a focus on game-ready optimization without sacrificing visual quality.
